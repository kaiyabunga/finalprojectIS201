  1. Monkey Facts
    • Monkeys are super smart — some use tools like sticks to get insects or stones to crack nuts.
    • They live in complex social groups with leaders, alliances, and even drama (monkey soap operas are real).
    • Monkeys live in Asia, Africa, and Central/South America — but not naturally in Australia or Europe.
    • Tree-lovers (like spider monkeys) are called arboreal, while ground-dwellers (like baboons) are terrestrial.
    • Howler monkeys are the loudest land animals — you can hear them from 3 miles away!
    • Capuchin monkeys wash their hands and even use leaves as cups.
